1. Overview

PropertyLime ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our property management application.

PropertyLime is a simple property management system designed to help you manage residents, inventory, tickets, expenses, and salaries. We follow data minimization principles and collect only the essential information needed for the app to function.

By using PropertyLime, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. You maintain full control over your data and can delete your account at any time.

2. Information We Collect

PropertyLime collects only the minimal information necessary to provide our property management services:

2.1 Account Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, login, and essential service communications
  • Property Name: The name of your property for identification within the app
  • Property Logo: Optional logo image to personalize your property profile

2.2 Property Management Data

All data you enter into the app (residents, inventory, tickets, expenses, salaries) is stored locally on your device or your chosen storage location. We do not access, collect, or store this information on our servers.

3. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can view your account information (email, property name, logo) in your account settings
  • Update: You can change your email, property name, or logo at any time in your account
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated information
  • Export: You can export your property management data from the app (stored locally on your device)

To delete your account or if you need help, please contact us using the information in the Contact section.
