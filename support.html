Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about PropertyLime

How do I get started with PropertyLime?

Getting started is easy! Download the PropertyLime app from the App Store or Google Play, create your account, and follow the setup wizard. You can start by adding your first property and inviting team members. Our onboarding process will guide you through the key features.

Can I manage multiple properties with PropertyLime?

Yes! PropertyLime is designed to handle multiple properties efficiently. You can switch between properties, view consolidated reports, and manage different teams for each property. There's no limit to the number of properties you can manage.

Is my data secure and backed up?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security with end-to-end encryption, secure cloud storage, and regular automated backups. Your data is stored in multiple secure locations and is protected by industry-standard security measures. We're also GDPR compliant.

Can I export my data from PropertyLime?

No, not right now but this feautur might be avaiable in the future.

How does the inventory management feature work?

The inventory feature allows you to track all property assets, maintenance supplies, and equipment. You can add items with photos, set reorder points, track usage, and generate reports. The system can also send alerts when items are running low.

Is there a mobile app for PropertyLime?

Yes, PropertyLime is available as a native mobile app for both iOS and Android devices. The mobile app includes all the features of the web version, with offline capabilities for essential functions. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play.

What if I need help that's not covered in the FAQ?

Our support team is here to help! You can contact us via email, phone, or through the in-app support chat. We typically respond to emails within 24 hours and offer phone support during business hours. For urgent issues, we provide priority support.
